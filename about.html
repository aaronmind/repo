A new company with a long history.

My father, Karl Keegan set up KJK Life & Pensions in 1981 after already spending 20 years in insurance services.
To allow him retire and ensure the continuance of service for our clients, Fusion Financial Planning was established and received authorisation as an insurance intermediary from the Central Bank of Ireland, in 2020.


It has been great to work with Karl over the past number of years, especially to appreciate how well regarded he is with his clients. Fusion Financial Planning Ltd is committed to continuing this level of service both for existing clients and new. Having grown up with KJK Life and Pensions, I have seen how a family’s financial needs change and evolve over time.


Fusion Financial Planning, through mindmymoney.ie, is here to help guide you through your life journey.
