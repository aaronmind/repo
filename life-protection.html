Life Protection

Protecting your family with life insurance is one of the most important financial decisions you can make. It means your family could get a lump sum or a regular income if you die and no longer have your income to maintain their standard of living, security, education and happiness.

However, not every plan is the same and the issues around establishing the correct cover for each family is different, depending on:

  • Income earned.
  • What benefits are available from the State.
  • How long you need to cover the loss of income.
  • How long you need cover for.
  • Married or co-habiting couples.
  • Type of cover.
  • How much you can afford.
  • Flexibility and conversion option.

Mortgage Protection protects the bank and the loan it has provided. It will only reduce the monthly expenses by the mortgage repayment.
